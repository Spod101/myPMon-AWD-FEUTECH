Ultimate Guide to Pokémon Starters

  • Grass-type Pokémon
  • Turtwig

Turtwig is a Grass-type Pokémon introduced in the Sinnoh region. It evolves into Grotle starting at level 18, which evolves into Torterra starting at level 32.

  • Fire-type Pokémon
  • Chimchar

Chimchar is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in the Sinnoh region. It evolves into Monferno starting at level 14, which evolves into Infernape starting at level 36.

  • Water-type Pokémon
  • Piplup

Piplup is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in the Sinnoh region. It evolves into Prinplup starting at level 16, which evolves into Empoleon starting at level 36.
